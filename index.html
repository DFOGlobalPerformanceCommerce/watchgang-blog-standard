This is the Startup That Watch Lovers Have Been Waiting For

Watch lovers are in for a special treat. Watch Gang , in just it’s second year, has become the largest and most loved watch of the month club in the world. Watch Gang now has more than 20,000 active subscribers. Since Grammy/Academy Award Winner Jamie Foxx posted about them on Instagram, growth has absolutely exploded. Even Entourage star, Jeremy Piven has joined Watch Gang.

How It Works

You pay a monthly membership fee based on what tier of watch you choose and each month they send you a watch in the mail. Each month’s watch is a surprise, you don’t know what to expect. You select watch preferences when you sign up, that you can adjust at any time, and the watch you get each month is based off of them. They match the watches to your preferences as closely as possible, while still providing a good variety to ensure a full spectrum of watches for your collection.

Watch Gang has 3 tiers of watches for members to choose from, Original, Black, and Platinum Tiers. There is also a non-recurring extremely limited membership called Centum ($1,000), which is where they partner with a brand and offer a limited edition watch from their collection exclusively to Watch Gang members. These watches typically range in value from $1,500-$5,000.

Watch Gang Tiers:

  • Original ($29):

    mostly fashionable, well made watches from lesser known brands with Japanese quartz movements. The value of these watches range from $50-$150.

  • Black ($99):

    mostly Japanese or Swiss automatic movement watches from either premium micro-brands that had a great start on Kickstarter, or as well known as Citizen. The value of these watches range from $150-$500.

  • Platinum ($299):

    mostly Japanese or Swiss automatic movement super-luxury watches from premium micro-brands or large name brands. The value of these watches range from $500-$1,500.

Membership Bonuses

Arguably just as cool as the subscription is the bonuses that all Watch Gang Members receive. Every Friday, one lucky member wins a Rolex and every Tuesday, a TAG. Members from any tier are eligible to win the Rolex and the TAG watches. They host the giveaways live on all their social media channels, and also giveaway lots of other things like a free month’s sub, or points for The Wheel. The Wheel is another way Watch Gang rewards its members. Points are earned with each month’s membership, can be won, and also purchased. Depending on how many points you have and want to use, you can select the appropriate tier of wheel to play. You get to choose what watches go on the wheel, and you get to select higher tier watches to add to the wheel, so you have a chance to win a better watch. This is cool because you can get a watch you want and have a chance at winning something better. Also, Watch Gang often has flash sales where members can get great deals on watches until the supply is sold out.

Community

Watch Gang Exchange is their Facebook Group where members can Buy, Sell, or Trade anything relating to watches, whether it be bands, extra links, winders, or watches. This is one of the most active groups out there. If you aren’t happy with a watch you got during a given month, it is very easy to find someone who will gladly take it off your hands.

Customer Service is awesome. They take the saying “It’s the little things that count” to heart. They are very friendly and quick to respond.
